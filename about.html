About Marc Stogaitis

Principal Engineer at Google, and on the weekend I apply AI to do deep hockey analysis as the creator of Deep Hockey.

My name is Marc Stogaitis, and I'm a principal engineer at Google with a passion for combining advanced technology with the fast-paced world of hockey. On the ice and behind the keyboard, I love exploring how machine learning and AI can deepen our understanding of the game.

In my spare time I like to use machine learning and AI to do deep hockey analysis. I've built several ML tools to support that work, including a Deep Hockey Foundation model and vision models that track player positions on the ice.

These machine learning tools let me work with massive amounts of game data so that I can look at many different examples and fully understand what's the right thing to do in a particular situation. By studying countless scenarios, I can compare patterns and evaluate decisions.
